Biography

A defining voice in Haitian music

Roody Roodboy is one of Haiti's most celebrated contemporary artists, renowned for his powerful voice, captivating stage presence, and ability to blend emotion with infectious rhythms. Fusing Konpa, Afro-Caribbean, R&B, and modern pop influences, he has become a defining figure in Haitian music, earning millions of streams and a devoted fan base across Haiti and the global Haitian diaspora.

Known for delivering unforgettable live performances, Roody brings unmatched energy and authenticity to every stage. His catalog of hit songs, heartfelt lyrics, and dynamic performances have made him a sought-after headliner for concerts, festivals, cultural celebrations, private events, and international tours.

Whether performing for intimate audiences or thousands of fans, Roody creates an experience that connects people through music, culture, and passion — a reputation that has solidified him as one of the premier entertainers in the Haitian music industry.
